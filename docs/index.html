2022-01-25

Chapter 1 Overview of survey

This brief report provides an overview of the progress survey on the International Year pledges. In total, more than 352 pledges were submitted by a variety of pledgemakers. All pledges went through a peer review process and 306 pledges were published on the endchildlabour.org website.

The International Year team launched the survey on 26 November. To date (2022-01-25), 75 surveys were submitted corresponding to 24.51 % of all pledgemakers.
